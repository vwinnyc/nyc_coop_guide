Rent stabilization note: If your unit is in a building with 6+ apartments built before 1974 (or receiving 421-a / J-51 benefits), it may be rent-stabilized. Under HSTPA 2019, most deregulation pathways were eliminated. Annual increases are set by the NYC Rent Guidelines Board (RGBO) — typically 2 to 4%. If stabilization is possible, cap your rent growth input at 2 to 2.5% and confirm status via NYC DHCR before purchase.
